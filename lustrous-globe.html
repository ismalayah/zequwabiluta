The Lustrous Globe (Japanese: だいしらたま Great Pearl Orb) is an item introduced in Pokémon Legends: Arceus. It allows Palkia to change between its normal form and Origin Forme. It was a Key Item in Generation VIII.

In the core series games

Effect

In Pokémon Legends: Arceus, when the Lustrous Globe is used from the satchel on Palkia, it switches it between its normal form and Origin Forme.

In Pokémon Scarlet and Violet, if a Palkia is holding a Lustrous Globe, it changes to its Origin Forme. Additionally, it boosts Palkia's Dragon- and Water-type moves by 20%.
