J.J. Watt Parents: Meet J.J. Watts Mom Connie Watt, And Dad John Watt

Justin James Watt is an American football defensive end with the National Football League’s Arizona Cardinals. He attended the University of Wisconsin and was picked in the first round of the 2011 NFL Draft by the Houston Texans.

J. JWatt played ice hockey from the age of four to the age of thirteen, competing in competitions as far away as Canada and Germany. He stopped playing hockey because the weekly games interfered with his schedule and he couldn’t afford it. J.J Watt was also a football player throughout his childhood, and he got enamored with the sport when he was in fifth grade.

J.J Watt and the Arizona Cardinals agreed to a two-year, $28 million contract on March 1, 2021, with $23 million guaranteed.

J.J. Watt Parents: Meet J.J. Watt’s Mom Connie Watt, And Dad John Watt

Connie and John Watt are J.J Watt’s parents.

J.J Watt was born on March 22, 1989, in Waukesha, Wisconsin, to building operations vice president Connie and firefighter John Watt.

J.J.’s younger brothers, who also play for the Pittsburgh Steelers, are also NFL players. T. J. Watt plays outside linebacker, while Derek Watt is a fullback.
