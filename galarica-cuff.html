The Galarica Cuff (Japanese: ガラナツブレス Galanut Bracelet) is a type of evolution item introduced in Generation VIII.

Effect

Causes Galarian Slowpoke to evolve into Galarian Slowbro upon use. This consumes the Galarica Cuff.
