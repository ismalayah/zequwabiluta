Who is Erick Brian Colon?American-Cuban singer Erick Brian Colon was born in Havana, Cuba under the zodiac sign Capricorn on 3 January 2001. He’s perhaps known best for having been a member of the no longer active Latin American boy band CNCO, with other members having been Joel Pimentel, Zabdiel De Jesus, Christopher Velez and Richard Camacho. The boys met when they auditioned on the show “La Banda”, and were active in the music industry from 2015 through 2023; they were quite popular, and were nominated for six Premios Juventud Youth Awards, winning five, some of which were for Favorite Fan-Army, Favorite Twitter Celebrity and Producer’s Choice.
