Benjamin Actor

Benjamin became highly interested in acting after taking part in several school stage productions. The young actor was selected in October 2016 to play Henry in the Let the Right One In series, based on the novel by John Ajvide Lindqvist. However, TNT did not approve the pilot and did not continue with the series. Months later, Benjamin appeared in the final episode of the MTV series Teen Wolf titled, The Wolves of War playing Alec.

In November 2017 when Benjamin was selected to play the part of Marcus in Deadly Class, he had originally auditioned for the role of Billy. It was later on that his role was changed to that of Marcus when the producers found out that he was Hispanic
